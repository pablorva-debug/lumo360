How It Works

Three steps from job description to clarity

Step 01

It starts with the role

Upload your job description and Lumo360 identifies the key competencies required for success, turning them into a structured interview framework.

Step 02

It runs a real interview

Candidates join a conversation that adapts in real time. The AI follows the framework, asking relevant questions and probing deeper based on each answer.

Step 03

Clear, evidence-based evaluations

After each interview, see how the candidate performed across every competency. Each score is backed by evidence from the conversation.

FAQ

Common questions

What is Lumo360?

Lumo360 is an AI interviewing platform that conducts natural, adaptive conversations with candidates and provides hiring teams with fully transparent, evidence-backed evaluation reports — so every decision is defensible.

How is Lumo360 different from other AI hiring tools?

Unlike black-box AI screening tools, Lumo360 makes every evaluation decision fully traceable. Your team can see the exact transcript moments that led to each recommendation — no unexplained scores.

Does Lumo360 replace human interviewers?

No. Lumo360 handles scalable first-round screening so your team can focus their time on the most promising candidates. Human judgement remains central — we just make it faster and more consistent.
